About Us

Empowering Future Innovators

    We are a group of passionate students dedicated to empowering the next generation of learners. At Jabana Code Bootcamp, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to explore technology, even those who cannot access traditional education. Through our free and inclusive coding and web development programs, we aim to unlock potential, inspire creativity, and build skills that create lasting impact in our communities.
